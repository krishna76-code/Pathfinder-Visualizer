Breadth-First Search (BFS)

BFS is an algorithm for traversing or searching tree or graph data structures. It starts at a selected node, explores all neighbor nodes at the present depth prior to moving on to nodes at the next depth level. BFS guarantees the shortest path in an unweighted graph.
